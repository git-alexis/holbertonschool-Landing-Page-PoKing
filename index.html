Register now so you don't miss out any of our upcoming event


See the details of the event you wish to attend. Register to reserve your place. Just click on the Register button and you will appear on the list of registered participants, guaranteeing your place! If you can't make it, don't hesitate to unsubscribe by clicking on the Unregister button.




Find out where you stand in the event ranking and aim for the top


Would you like to know where you currently stand in a particular event and understand how you can progress from event to event? See how many points you have scored and how many players you have eliminated so you can adapt your game accordingly.




Optimise your strategies with our statistics


With our statistics calculator, obtaining probalities is simple! Enter your cards, click on the Get statistics button and let our tool do the rest. Instantly see valuable information to help you optimise your performance.







About PoKing

The idea for this project came from personal experience. As a member of a poker club in 2023, I noticed a few shortcomings in terms of operations. For example, registrations were managed manually on a sheet of paper, and rankings were calculated in Excel. I therefore felt it was necessary to automate these functions by creating a site that would make it easier for the club to manage events and their rankings.

The site took 6 weeks to build, with 1 week spent researching the documentation, 3 weeks setting up the back end and 2 weeks on the design.
